HOW TO USE:

A. Normal Backup:
   1. Plug in external drive (F:, G:, H:, or E:)
   2. Run QUANTUM_RECOVERY.bat
   3. Choose option (1-5)
   4. Wait for "Complete!"

B. Emergency Recovery:
   1. Plug in your backup drive
   2. Boot to Safe Mode (F8)
   3. Run QUANTUM_RECOVERY.bat
   4. Choose option 2 or 3

C. Backup Location:
   Your backups are on your external drive in:
   [DRIVE]:\GREG_QUANTUM_BACKUP\[TYPE]_[DATE]
   Example: F:\GREG_QUANTUM_BACKUP\quantum_master_20250203
