## Core Sacred Texts

### 1. Book of the Dead (Pert em hru)
- **Purpose**: Guide for the soul's journey through the afterlife
- **Origin**: New Kingdom period (c. 1550-1070 BCE)
- **Key Components**:
  - Spells for protection
  - Prayers to the gods
  - Instructions for divine judgment
  - Sacred knowledge of the afterlife

### 2. Pyramid Texts
- **Purpose**: Royal resurrection texts
- **Origin**: Old Kingdom (c. 2400-2300 BCE)
- **Location**: Inscribed in pyramids
- **Content**:
  - Ascension spells
  - Divine protection
  - Star alignment rituals
  - Royal transformation

### 3. Coffin Texts
- **Purpose**: Afterlife protection for nobles
- **Origin**: Middle Kingdom (c. 2000-1700 BCE)
- **Location**: Inscribed on coffins
- **Features**:
  - Protection spells
  - Divine knowledge
  - Sacred maps
  - Transformation rituals

### 4. Emerald Tablets
- **Purpose**: Hermetic wisdom
- **Attribution**: Thoth/Hermes
- **Content**:
  - Divine wisdom
  - Sacred science
  - Spiritual alchemy
  - Universal laws
